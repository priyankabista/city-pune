Pune: Colleges and Universities

Pune is home to 1390 well-known colleges in the country. These include MIT WPU - World Peace University, Symbiosis Institute of Business Management (SIBM Pune), Symbiosis Centre for Management & Human Resource Development (SCMHRD Pune), AIMS Institute of Management Studies, ICFAI Business School (IBS), Department of Management Sciences, Pune University (PUMBA), UNIPUNE - Savitribai Phule Pune University, College of Engineering, Pune (COEP), St. Mira's College For Girls, Ness Wadia College of Commerce, SNDT Women's University, etc.

Colleges in Pune: Eligibility Criteria

The essential eligibility criteria for admission to Colleges in Pune are:

Candidates must have passed Class 12

Candidates applying for BA, BCom, and BSc must have passed Class 12 with the minimum marks required for each course

The eligibility criteria vary depending on the candidate’s category and college

There is no age limit for UG programmes offered by the Colleges in Pune.
